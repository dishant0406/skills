# Feed Workflow

Use this file while sourcing posts from the feed.

## Discovery rules

1. Start from the home feed.
2. Scroll before acting; do an initial pass across multiple screenfuls so you are not choosing only from the first visible posts.
3. Never use search for this workflow unless the user explicitly overrides that rule.
4. Open only posts that are relevant to the requested topic set.
5. Prefer posts with a clear claim, launch, opinion, demo, or lesson.
6. Open posts only by clicking from the feed; do not visit post URLs directly.
7. Back out after reviewing the post and continue sourcing the next candidate from the feed.

## Selection bar

Pick posts that are:

- Fresh
- Specific
- Worth adding to
- Aligned with the user's requested topic focus

Skip posts that are:

- Too vague to comment on intelligently
- Pure engagement bait
- Off-topic
- Duplicate takes on the same thing unless the angle is genuinely different
- Consecutive near-identical takes in a row when a different relevant topic is available

## Cadence

CRITICAL: do not pick all `N` posts first and then comment in a batch.
CRITICAL: scroll, find one post, open it, comment, return to the feed, scroll more, then find the next one.
CRITICAL: use strict sequence: click post in feed -> read -> comment -> back to feed -> scroll -> click next post.
CRITICAL: do not keep multiple candidate posts open and hop between them.
CRITICAL: do not refresh the feed between comments while working toward `N` unless the site is broken and the user explicitly wants that fallback.
